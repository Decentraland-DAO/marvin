Description

This proposal is to extend the operations of the DAO Facilitation Squad, for an additional six months. Over the course of its first term, the DAO Facilitation Squad developed a cadence of operations and regular reporting, and identified core areas where our engagement is most useful, this includes but is not limited to:

  1. Hosting of DAO Town Halls, In-World Meet Ups and DAO Facilitation Office Hours;
  2. Strategic Communications and Reporting on DAO Operations and Governance issues;
  3. Development of a Formal Working Group Process and Facilitation of Issue-Specific Working Groups;
  4. Coordination of engagements and collaborations between the Decentraland DAO Community, DAO Core Units, the DAO Committee, and the Decentraland Foundation.

The operations of the DAO Facilitation Squad are further intended to support the work and community engagement of other Core Units of the DAO, which include the DAO Governance (dApp) Squad and the Grants Support Squad.

Our primary objective over the coming term will be to focus on our DAOs collective decision-making and consensus building processes. Many in the web3 space often equate governance to voting. However, over the past months we have seen that voting is often not the end, but the beginning of a divisive issue or debate, and that collective decision-making and consensus building is as, if not more important, than the final outcome of a proposal or poll.

Drawing on leading policy decision-making frameworks, including what is known as the Delphi Method, we will focus on finalizing and implementing a robust working group process that can be led by Community and/or Facilitation Team Members; and further focus on creating and maintaining multiple forums for increasing communication, coordination, and collaboration between the DAO Community, Committee, Core Units and Decentraland Foundation.

ROAD MAP & MILESTONES (FULL)

Our primary objective continues to be facilitating development of our DAOs collective decision-making and consensus building processes. This will include finalization and oversight of the DAOs working group processes, as well as coordinating distributed strategy and vision alignment exercises across and within stakeholder groupings, including the DAO Community, DAO Core Units, and DAO Committee.

Direct deliverables to support this process include:

  1. 24x Weekly DAO Brief: Weekly report published on Sunday of each week, describing DAO operations and governance issues. (4 reports/month; 24 reports total)
  2. 6x Monthly Community Pulse: In depth strategic governance, creator spotlight, and issue analysis report. (1 report/month; 6 reports total)
  3. 12x Biweekly Town Hall: Twice monthly town halls hosted by DAO Facilitation team alternating between US and EU/ASIA friendly time zones. (2x town halls/month; 12 town halls total)
  4. 24x Weekly Facilitation Hours: Weekly in-world or discord-based office hours for open discussion between DAO Facilitators and Community Members. (4 facilitation hours/month; 24 facilitations hours total)
  5. 6x Monthly Twitter Space AMA: Monthly DAO Facilitation Team Twitter Space AMAs. (1 twitter space/month; 6 twitter spaces total)
  6. Persistent Social Media Management and Engagement: Operation and posting of regular content on the DCL DAO Twitter and Youtube accounts. For a full description of the Strategic Relations role and road map can be found here.
  7. Ad-Hoc Facilitation of issue-specific working groups.

Examples of previous deliverables and all future relevant deliverables will be published on the DCL DAO Mirror page, which is Managed by the Facilitation Team.

Finally, the DAO Facilitation Team is committed to stewarding the development of core strategic alignment, and specific strategic documents - should the latter be the wish of the DAO Community. Core Units of the DAO recently launched a proposal to create a distributed strategic alignment process, and should this process be approved, the DAO Facilitation Team will make it a core operational priority to moderate, mediate and support advancement of this process.

In the absence of community consensus to develop strategic alignment documents, the Facilitation team will support development and drafting of a limited set of such documents, outlying the operational and strategic alignment of the three Core Units of the DAO: The Governance Squad, Grants Support Squad, and Facilitation Team.

Ultimately, the DAO Facilitation Team will have a persistent presence in the community, and serve as an outlet for and support expression of community and other stakeholder interests. This expression can take many forms, foremost of which is in actionable and implementable governance proposals.
